--- No command or help: shows help section 
ex: 'node index.js' or 'node index.js help'

--- List: shows current to-dos, or shows an appropriate text if there are no to-dos.
ex: 'node index.js list'

--- Add: adds a to-do item. All the words behind add must be a string and are entered as 1 to-do item to the list.
ex: 'node index.js add "Buy groceries"' 

--- Remove: removes a to-do item by its 1-base index, e.g. to remove second item, 
ex: 'node index.js remove 2'

--- Reset: removes all to-do items from the list. 
ex: 'node index.js reset'
